Our Chapter

The Tau Kappa chapter of Phi Gamma Delta (FIJI) has been one of the best-regarded fraternities at the University of Toronto since 1923.

Mission

Our fraternity exists to promote lifelong friendships, to reaffirm high ethical standards and values, and to foster personal development in the pursuit of excellence.


Phi Gamma Delta is committed to provide opportunities for each brother to develop responsibility, leadership, scholarship, and social skills in order to become a fully contributing member of society.

"Phi Gamma Delta helps university students build life skills not otherwise taught at university.
Lifelong friendships, leadership excellence, responsibility, and high moral character are the hallmarks of our chapter."

values

In pursuit of our mission, the Fraternity promotes five core values:

Friendship

The sweetest influence. It is what brings us together, forming the basis of brotherhood.

knowledge

The primary reason we attend university, and the key to a fuller and richer life.

Service

In recognition of our obligation to give back to the campus and community that foster our growth.

morality

Our commitment to do what is right in all our relationships.

excellence

Transcendence of the ordinary, and defying complacency. We are dedicated to the pursuit of the achievement of the full potential of both the chapter and each individual member.
